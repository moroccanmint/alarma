Your Flood Alert and Safe Route Navigation System

About the Project

ALARMA is an innovative flood monitoring and navigation system designed to help communities stay safe during flood events. By combining real-time flood monitoring with intelligent route planning, ALARMA ensures users can navigate safely through their city while avoiding flooded areas.

Key Features

Real-time Monitoring

Get instant updates from our network of flood monitoring stations

Safe Route Planning

Automatically calculate routes that avoid flooded areas

Early Warning System

Receive timely alerts about flooding in your area

How It Works

1

Our monitoring stations continuously collect water level data

2

The system analyzes the data to identify flood risks

3

Users receive alerts and can find safe routes through the city
